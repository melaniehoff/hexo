Doppelcam is a visually-similar camera in the form of a website and ios app. It is a tool for decontextualizing your surroundings and glimpsing the uncanny valley. A photo taken with Doppelcam is replaced by an image pulled from the internet that is similar to the one just taken in pixel-by-pixel value.
