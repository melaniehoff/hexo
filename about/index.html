Melanie Hoff


My practice is research-based and site-specific. I use technology and computation to play with systems that mediate and control society and believe in installation that moves away from art-contexts and into the processes of everyday life. My work investigates conventions, language, and the ways in which representation actively shapes our interactions and consciousness.
